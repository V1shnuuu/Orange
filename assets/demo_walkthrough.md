# CirclePact Demo Walkthrough

This walkthrough highlights the core CirclePact experience from onboarding to circle creation and analytics review.

## 1. Home and onboarding
- Open the live app at https://circlepact-mvp.vercel.app
- Review the responsive landing experience and navigation

## 2. Circle creation and participation
- Create or explore savings circles
- Review contribution flow, cycle timing, and payout expectations

## 3. Analytics and feedback
- Inspect protocol analytics and reputation signals
- Use the feedback modal to share product feedback
