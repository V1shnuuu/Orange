This document summarizes the validation work completed for the CirclePact MVP submission, including onboarding evidence, wallet interaction coverage, and feedback collection.

## 1. MVP Validation Scope
The CirclePact MVP was reviewed across the core experience areas:
- wallet onboarding and connection
- circle creation and participation
- contribution and payout flow
- analytics and feedback touchpoints
- responsive UI behavior on desktop and mobile
